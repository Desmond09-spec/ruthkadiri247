Welcome to the Movies page
Here is a quick guide on what you need to know:

  • You may watch RuthKadiri247 movies directly on this page. However, downloads are not available here due to YouTube’s Terms of Service. Instead, you’ll be redirected to a third-party site where the movie link will be prefilled. Once it loads and processes, simply click the provided download button.

  • A menu bar will appear at the top of this page once this modal is dismissed. From there, you will be able to navigate to the homepage, filter the movies by latest, most popular, or oldest, and search through the movies. However, for now, you may only search for a movie using its name.

    Note: The menu bar title reflects the active filter. The 'Latest' filter is applied by default each time the page loads.

  • Each movie displays a download icon on the left and a bookmark icon on the right, both vertically centered. You can click the download icon to download a movie. The bookmark feature is not yet active, but will be available soon. The visibility of these icons can be managed from the menu bar.
You may need to scroll down to see the menu title in the top-right corner.
